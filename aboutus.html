About LARS

Timeline of Artifical Intelligence

From grading papers to tutoring students, Lars has multiple uses in the business and educational industry. This reprogrammable AI is able to grading papers, translate up to 20 languages, can adapt to different student learning styles (e.g. visual, audio), tutor students, and create lesson plans. These services can be used in schools through educating students and large-scale companies through training employees. Not to mention, it's reprogrammable through the LARS app which comes with a free whitelist after payment.
